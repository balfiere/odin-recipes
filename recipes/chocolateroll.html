Single Serving Chocolate Roll

Description

A chocolate twist on a classic cinnamon roll, just enough for one serving. Vegan with gluten-free option. Recipe by Mini Batch Baker.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/4 cup flour (30 g) gf all purpose if desired
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp oil (5 g)
  • 1 tsp maple syrup (7 g)
  • 1 tbsp (nut) milk (15 g)

Chocolate Filling

  • 2 tsp nut/seed butter (10 g)
  • 1 tsp maple syrup (7 g)
  • 1/2 tsp cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and grease a 1/2 cup ramekin or any baking dish really.
  2. In a small bowl add filling ingredients and mix until smooth. Set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together flour and baking powder.
  4. Add oil, syrup, milk and combine to make a non-sticky dough (dust a bit more flour if needed).
  5. Knead a few times until you get a smooth ball of dough. Spread out into a long thin rectangle.
  6. Smear the filling, (optional) sprinkle cinnamon. Roll it up, transfer to ramekin, bake 20 mins.
  7. Remove from the oven, serve and enjoy!
